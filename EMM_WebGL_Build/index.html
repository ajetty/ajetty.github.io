Controls

  • WASD to pan camera
  • Q to rotate camera to the left
  • E to rotate camera to the right
  • R to have camera zoom in
  • F to have camera zoom out
  • Please use mouse to select a purple astronaut
    • when pressing its move button you will see yellow selection squares - that is the character's movement range
    • when close to a lizard (a Martian/Enemy) press the attack button - if its on a yellow square then its within range of attack

The first team whose character's Health reaches zero loses.
The game is played in rounds with the purple astronauts, Elon's Musketeers, going first.
With each turn a musketeer has, their turn diamond will go from green to red to show who has had their turn.
Once everyone has had their turn then the Martians will take theirs and then starts another round.
